Instructions for Using the Grade Checker:

Enter a Number: Input a number between 1 and 9 (inclusive) to check your corresponding grade.

Understand the Grades: Each number corresponds to a specific grade:
1 = A1 | 2 = B2 | 3 = B3 | 4 = C4 | 5 = C5 | 6 = C6| 7 = D7 | 8 = E8 | 9 = F8.

Receive Your Grade: Entering your numbers to the input fields, the system will automatically calculate and give to instant feedback.

Invalid Input: If you enter a number outside the range of 1 to 9, you will receive an error message: "Invalid input. Please enter a number between 1 and 9."
